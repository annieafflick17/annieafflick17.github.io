About Me

Film Student · Editor · Sound Designer

I am a third year film student studying at SCAD with a passion for storytelling through picture and sound.

I've worked on both narrative and documentary projects, with a particular interest in sound effects editing for narrative work, and the ways sound builds atmosphere and emotion.

Alongside my sound work, I am drawn to documentary filmmaking, centered around endangered species and environmental conservation. My connection to nature has always been a big presence in my life – from picking up snakes I found in my backyard as a kid, to developing an even deeper appreciation for wildlife through film. This connection is a great influence on the stories I want to tell, further developed through my experience creating documentary work abroad.

Through my work in editing and sound, I strive to create stories that will evoke emotion and leave a lasting impression.
